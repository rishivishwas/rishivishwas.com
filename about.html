About Adv. Rishi Vishwas

Adv. Rishi Vishwas
High Court Criminal Advocate | Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh, India

Adv. Rishi Vishwas, born on 11th November 1995 in Pratapgarh, Uttar Pradesh, India, is a dedicated and professional High Court Criminal Advocate practicing at the Allahabad High Court. He specializes in criminal defense, FIR management, bail applications, revision petitions, and writ petitions.

Known for his client-focused approach, strong advocacy skills, and thorough understanding of criminal law, Adv. Rishi Vishwas provides effective and reliable legal representation to individuals seeking justice. His professional services are guided by integrity, diligence, and a commitment to ensuring clear and practical solutions for every client.
